SSD Bonanza

2010/12/08

I've finally found the time and the force of will to backup everything and to upgrade my MacBook with an SSD disk and some RAM. Crucial had a nice sale last month to which I could not resist.

I got rid of the crappy superdrive and moved the old hard disk in a caddy found on ebay. An heartfelt goodbye message to my superdrive: DIE YOU BASTARD!

Now I have 64gb of SSD bonanza for MacOS and Virtual Machines and 140gb for storage. Can't be more happy.

Since I had to re-install everything I was almost thinking of installing FreeBSD but memories of past experiences stopped me. At now the way to go on a MacBook is MacOS and VM for other needs.
